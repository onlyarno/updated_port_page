About Me

Anyway, I am a graduate of UMKC with a biology and chemistry major, although i like biology a tad bit more. Presently, I hope to become a full-stack web-developer, and perhaps combine the two knowledges together.

In off time, I like to relax, play the piano, socialize with closest friends, watch movies, play video games, and other things. I also love my dog more than anything else.
